{left margin} Juan Francisco In this parish of Taos on the tenth day of the month of December of one thousand eight hundred and forty-three: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a child, one month old. I named him Juan Francisco, descendant of the Ute tribe from which he was rescued. He is a servant of Antonio Díaz Armenta and María Isabel Sánchez. His godparents were Juan Antonio Madrid and María Manuela Romero, all residents of the town of Río Colorado. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
