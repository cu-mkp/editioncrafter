{left margin} José Miguel In this parish of Taos on the third day of the month of November of one thousand eight hundred and forty-one: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a child, five years old. I gave him the name José Miguel, an Indian rescued from the Ute tribe. He is a servant of Juan Antonio Abeyta, and his godfather was Pablo Garduño, both residents of the town of Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe. I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
