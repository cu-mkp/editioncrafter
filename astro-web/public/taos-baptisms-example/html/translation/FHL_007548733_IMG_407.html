{left margin} María Soledad In this parish of Taos on the twenty-fourth day of the month of March of one thousand eight hundred and forty-five: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a two-day-old child. I named her María Soledad. She is the natural daughter of María Guadalupe Bent, an Indian servant of Charles Bent, who had been rescued from the nations of the north. The godparents were Elfego Bent and María Romualda Luna, residents of the town of Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
