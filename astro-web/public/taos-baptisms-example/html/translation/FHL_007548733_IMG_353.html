{left margin} José Matías In this parish of Taos on the third day of the month of October of one thousand eight hundred and forty-four: Having carried out the corresponding catechisms until found to be well-instructed in the principles, means, and ends of our holy religion, I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly exorcised, baptized, placed holy oils and sacred chrism on an adult, eight years old, according to the rite of our Holy Mother Church for the baptism of adults. I named him José Matías. He is a servant of Fernando Trujillo and Juana María Trujillo, residents of the town of Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe. The same were also his godparents. The said adult is a descendant of the Ute tribe from which he was rescued. I advised the said godparents and masters of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric} {left margin} María Antonia In this parish of Taos on the third day of the month of October of one thousand eight hundred and forty-four: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a child, four years old. I named her María Antonia. She is Indian, descendant of the Ute tribe from which she was rescued. She is a servant of Doña María Rafaela Varela, resident of the town of Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe. The godparents were Pablo Valdez and María Magdalena Gallegos, residents of the same place. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
