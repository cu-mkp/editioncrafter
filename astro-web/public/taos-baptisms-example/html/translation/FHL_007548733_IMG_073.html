{left margin} José Rafael In this parish of Taos on the fifth day of the month of August of the year 1839: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a six-day-old child. I named him José Rafael, natural son of Guadalupe Vigil, an Indian servant of the late José Vigil, resident of the town of La Santísima Trinidad de Arroyo Seco. His godparents were Francisco Tomás [Bentos?] and María Soledad Vigil, residents of the same place. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
