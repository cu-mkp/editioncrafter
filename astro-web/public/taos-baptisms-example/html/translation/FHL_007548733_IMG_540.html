{left margin} María Rosalía In this parish of Taos on the twentieth day of the month of June of one thousand eight hundred and forty-six: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a child, five years old. I named her María Rosalía, descendant of the Navajo tribe from which she was rescued. She is a servant of Francisco Salazar and María Manuela Vigil, residents of the town of La Santísima Trinidad. The godparents were Luciano Salazar and María de la Concepción Salazar, residents of the same place. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
