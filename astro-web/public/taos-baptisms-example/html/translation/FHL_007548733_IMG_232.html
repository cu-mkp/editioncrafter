{left margin} María Florentina In this parish of Taos on the sixteenth day of the month of October of one thousand eight hundred and forty-two: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a one-day-old infant. I named her María Florentina, natural daughter of an Indian servant of Señora Ignacia Jaramillo, resident of the town of Nuestra Señora de Guadalupe. The godparents were Francisco Jaramillo and María Romualda de Luna, residents of the same place. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
