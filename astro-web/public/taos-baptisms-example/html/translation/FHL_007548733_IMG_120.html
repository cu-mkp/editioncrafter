{left margin} María Gabriela In this parish of Taos on the thirteenth day of the month of August of one thousand eight hundred and forty: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized a child, two years old, whom I named María Gabriela, rescued from the Paiute nation and servant of José Gabriel Gallegos. Her godparents were José Nazario Gallegos and María Antonia Gallegos, residents of Arroyo Seco. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship. I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
