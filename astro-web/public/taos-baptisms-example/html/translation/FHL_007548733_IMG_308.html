{left margin} María Antonia In this parish of Taos on the fourteenth day of the month of April of one thousand eight hundred and forty-four: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on an adult, thirteen years old, of the Navajo tribe. I named her María Antonia, servant of Miguel Mascareñas, both residents of the town of Santa Gertrudis.
