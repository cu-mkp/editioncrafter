{left margin} María Margarita In this parish of Taos on the first day of the month of March of one thousand eight hundred and forty-six: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez solemnly baptized and placed holy oils and sacred chrism on a child, two years old. I named her María Margarita. A descendant of the Navajo tribe from which she was rescued. She is a servant of José Dolores Córdoba and María Ramona Martínez, residents of the town of Sangre de Cristo. The godparents were José Felipe Nerio Maes and María del Refugio Pacheco, residents of the same place. I advised them of their obligation and spiritual kinship, and I signed it for the record. Antonio José Martínez {rubric}
