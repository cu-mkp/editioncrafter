Antonio José Martínez {rubric} {left margin} José de la Cruz In this parish of Taos on the second day of the month of February of the year one thousand eight hundred and forty-two: I, parish priest Don Antonio José Martínez, received a request from the previous year to become a Christian from an adult male, 30 years old, descendant of the Jicarilla tribe. I had advised him that he should be instructed in the knowledge of the principal mysteries of our holy faith and religion, and in the commandments or precepts that Christians accept as their obligation, and the motives for wanting to become a Christian, warning him that the main purpose was to serve God in this life and afterward, to see Him, and to enjoy in the blessedness that He has for his chosen ones in the hereafter. As of this date, he had completed the corresponding catechisms.
