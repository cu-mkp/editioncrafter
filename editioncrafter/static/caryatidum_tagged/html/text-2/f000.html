Of many-shaped caryatids (which common people call terms) or atlantids clothed in white, adapted to any order of architecture whatsoever, used during the first century, skillfully invented of this art. Many diverse terms according to the five orders of building for the benefit of all sculptors and stonemasons, joiners, glass etchers, and all artistic craftspeople or all those who love antique frame decorations. Invented by Hans Vreedeman de Vries. Published by Gerard de Jode.
